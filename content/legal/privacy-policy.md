This Privacy Policy describes how **e8e.dev (“we”, “our”, “us”)** collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

We collect your email address only when you voluntarily subscribe to receive updates through our email newsletter, which is powered by **Kit (ConvertKit)**.

We do **not** collect:

* payment information
* personal profile information
* sensitive personal data

---

## **2. How We Use Your Information**

Your email address is used solely to:

* send blog updates or newsletters you opted into
* contact you with occasional announcements or related updates

We **never sell, rent, or share** your email address with third parties.

---

## **3. Third-Party Services**

We use **Kit (ConvertKit)** to manage email subscriptions.
When you submit your email, their **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service** apply.

---

## **4. Cookies & Analytics**

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors use our site.
Google Analytics may collect non-personally identifiable data such as:

* IP address
* browser type/device
* pages visited

This data helps us improve user experience.
You may disable cookies or block Google Analytics using your browser settings or tools like Google’s opt-out extension.

---

## **5. Your Rights**

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EU under GDPR), you may request:

* access to your data
* correction or deletion
* withdrawal from all emails at any time via the unsubscribe link
